Happy 75th Independence day

India is all set to celebrate its 75th Independence Day on August 15. The entire country is observing this historic milestone by taking part in the 'Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav' to commemorate the 75 years of independence. India got freedom from British colonial rule after 200 years on August 15, 1947.

Government has launched the 'Har Ghar Tiranga' campaign from August 13 to August 15 and Independence Week from August 11 to August 17 to celebrate the 75 years of India's independence.
